# Field Operations Guide

## For Community Health Workers

### Receiving Alerts

Alerts arrive via:
- SMS
- WhatsApp
- Mobile app notification

### Alert Format
⚠️ HIGH RISK: Flood in Mora
- 12,000 under-5 children affected
- 3,200 children with disabilities
- Cholera risk: HIGH (poor WASH)
### Actions:
- Distribute ORS packets
- Check water sources
- Report cases immediately
- Contact: Dr. Marie (222-231-300)

### Responding to Alerts

1. Acknowledge receipt
2. Assess local situation
3. Take recommended actions
4. Report outcomes
